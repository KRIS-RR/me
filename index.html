About the Apartment

A Modern Retreat in the Heart of Halkidiki

Welcome to A&A Modern Residence, a thoughtfully designed holiday apartment that combines contemporary elegance with Mediterranean warmth.

The apartment features two beautifully appointed bedrooms — a master with a double bed and a second with two single beds, perfect for families or friends. The bright, airy living spaces maximize natural light.

The fully equipped kitchen makes self-catering effortless, while the private balcony is the perfect spot for morning coffee among the olive trees.
